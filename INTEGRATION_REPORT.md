## 🎯 **Unified Strategic Recommendations**

### **🔥 Primary Investment: Creatine (60% Allocation)**

#### **Why Now?**
- **Structural growth confirmed** (15-20% CAGR over 5 years)
- **Recent acceleration** (+28.7% YoY momentum)
- **Underserved female segment** (minimal ad targeting)
- **Seasonal opportunities** (January + July peaks)

#### **Action Plan:**
1. **Increase media spend** by 40% immediately
2. **Launch female-focused campaigns** (identity-based hooks)
3. **Prioritize UGC creators** over branded content
4. **Bundle with protein** for cross-selling opportunities
5. **Time campaigns for January & July peaks**

### **🛡️ Secondary Investment: Protein (25% Allocation)**

#### **Why Maintain?**
- **Stable, predictable growth** (3-5% CAGR)
- **Low volatility** (safe investment)
- **Strong correlation with creatine** (cross-selling potential)
- **Foundational supplement** status

#### **Action Plan:**
1. **Optimize current campaigns** (don't scale aggressively)
2. **Bundle with creatine** for higher AOV
3. **Focus on recovery messaging** (complements performance)
4. **Maintain seasonal timing** (January + September)

### **🎯 Tertiary Investment: Omega 3 (15% Allocation)**

#### **Why Test?**
- **Accelerating growth** (+16.9% recent vs 8-12% historical)
- **Low competition** (opportunity space)
- **Health positioning** (complements fitness trend)
- **Moderate volatility** (managed risk)

#### **Action Plan:**
1. **Test educational campaigns** (brain health, inflammation)
2. **Target health-conscious beginners** (Persona 3)
3. **Focus on July seasonal peak**
4. **Monitor correlation with protein** for bundling

### **🚫 Phase Out: Weight Loss (0% Allocation)**

#### **Why Exit?**
- **Structural decline confirmed** (-5% CAGR over 5 years)
- **Accelerating decline** (-18.2% recent YoY)
- **High competition** with declining demand
- **Negative momentum** worsening

#### **Action Plan:**
1. **Immediately pause new campaigns**
2. **Reposition existing inventory** toward performance
3. **Monitor for potential turnaround** (unlikely)
4. **Reallocate budget to creatine**
